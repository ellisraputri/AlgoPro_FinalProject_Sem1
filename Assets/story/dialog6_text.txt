After the battle, the vampire's body dispersed into dust.
And, the wind gradually flew all her remains away.
"Now, all things have ended. Rest in peace."
The boy sighed. A quite long sigh. 
"Looks like I need to find a new home."
"Hm, where should I go now? East or west?"
The boy pondered for a while.
"Hmm, let's just go east then."
"I heard the mountain there is beautiful."
He packed his things into an old bag he found in the forest.
"Okay, all set, let's go."
The boy smiled as he started his new journey.
